About Me

I currently earn a living writing code, but in the past I've worked as a market researcher, a tennis instructor, and even pruned some trees.

In college I started (and sold) a company that generated cash by renting appliances to facilitate international microloans to Third World entrepreneurs.

I also created and grew a community of South Asian student organizations for 3 years.

I have a degree in Finance, but I've forgotten most of what I learned. Some of the more valuable parts of my education come from the 8 years I spent at the Maharishi School of the Age of Enlightenment.
