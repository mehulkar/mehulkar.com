About Me

I currently earn a living writing code, but I also teach tennis. In the past I've worked as a market researcher, washed dishes and pruned trees.

I also created and grew a community of South Asian student organizations for 3 years.

I have a degree in Finance, but I've forgotten most of what I learned. Some of the more valuable parts of my education come from the 8 years I spent at the Maharishi School of the Age of Enlightenment.
